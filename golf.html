Our Courses

Sea View Course

Our signature course, there will be water hazards, bunkers thoughtfully placed, and panoramic coastal views that will challenge even the most seasoned players.

Woodlands Course

This tranquil course is perfect for golfers who are looking for a game that is both tough and relaxing. It is bordered with mature trees and provides moderate hills.

Valley Course

It is a scenic course that contains natural water features, rolling hills, and broad fairways that are suitable for players of all ability levels.

Golf Lessons

Digital Coaching
