Our Facilities

Hotel Accommodation

Luxurious suites that offer amazing views of the ocean and the fairway are available for your enjoyment. With our rooms, you can be sure that your stay will be both pleasant and luxurious.

Restaurants & Café

Indulge your taste buds at one of our on-site eateries or cafés. We provide a wide variety of cuisines, ranging from regional delicacies to cuisines from around the world.

Pro Shop

Discover our extensive selection of golf gear, including the newest equipment, clothes, and accessories, at our pro store.

Our Clubhouses

Main Clubhouse

Our main clubhouse is the perfect place for golfers and their visitors to unwind and mingle, with its cozy lounges, delicious eating options, and friendly ambiance.

Sea View Clubhouse

With views of the famous Sea View course, this clubhouse offers great dining, private event areas, and a wide view of the ocean.
